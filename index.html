The Smart Sustainable City of KL

Smart city is envisaged to have four pillars. Its Social, Physical, Institutional and Economic Infrastructure. The centre of attention for each of these pillars is the citizen.

For all of us to survive and prosper, we need new, intelligent urban planning that creates safe, affordable and resilient cities with green and culturally inspiring living conditions.

About us

We provide a portal for people to join communities that want to innovate the city and a platform that raises awareness and provides solutions that meet the needs of present and future generations with respect to economic, social, environmental as well as cultural aspects.

Our Goals

Making governance citizen-friendly and cost effective
Rely on online services to bring about accountability and transparency, especially using mobiles to reduce cost of services and providing services without having to go to municipal offices; use online monitoring of programs and activities with the aid of cyber tour of worksites.
Promote zero use of carbon emission vehicles
Constructing wider and safer walkaways and bicycle lanes. When possible, walk or ride your bike in order to avoid carbon emissions completely. Carpooling and public transportation drastically reduce CO2 emissions. Promote sales of a low carbon vehicle.
Preserving and developing open spaces
Building more parks, playgrounds, and recreational spaces in order to enhance the quality of life and reduce the urban heat effects and generally promote eco-balance.
Applying Smart Solutions
Developing a smart waste management system to make their collection processes more efficient. Using IoT sensors to collect air quality data for a smart air quality sensor.
